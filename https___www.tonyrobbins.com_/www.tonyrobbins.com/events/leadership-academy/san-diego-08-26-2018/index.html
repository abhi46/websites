AWAKEN THE LEADER IN YOU

Leadership Academy provides you with 30 years of expertise in four powerful days of total immersion. Learn tools, strategies and secrets Tony Robbins consistently uses to create immediate impact and compel lasting, positive change in others. You will learn what motivates people, how to harness the power of influence in yourself and others to achieve a greater vision for mankind at every level. Imagine being capable to step into any situation and make a real difference in the quality of not only your life, but the lives of others. In times of uncertainty, crisis and change, the world needs skilled leaders like never before.

Get Tickets

Discover the Leader Within

All great leaders possess a similar psychology. Learn the different types of leaders and discover your own strengths. Gain the tools to break through your limitations and step out as a leader.

Motivate and Inspire

Leaders are turned to in times of uncertainty. Gain the confidence required to inspire others as you learn the 3 mandates of leadership. Discover what motivates people and tools to create change.

Deliver with Confidence

Leadership begins with your ability to persuade and connect. Learn to engage and captivate any audience from beginning to end for a powerful, lasting impact.

Psychotherapist/NLP Expert | The power of Neuro-Linguistic Programming

Sid Jacobson
Psychotherapist/NLP Expert | The power of Neuro-Linguistic Programming

All understanding of people, and how they change and grow, begins with skilled observation. Sid Jacobson is an internationally recognized expert in Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), the state of the art in helping people. For over 35 years Sid has worked as a researcher, trainer, and consultant to professionals and organizations all over the world. He holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology and is also the author of five books on education and business, and the co-author of another on writing. He currently consults and trains people in counseling, coaching, business and personal development. Sid will help you more finely tune your observational skills to new levels, explore some of the inner workings of yourself and others, and create deeper level lasting change.

Author of Let Me See Your Body Talk and Strictly Business Body Language, Jan has worked with thousands in the field of personal growth and self-expression via seminars and workshops for the past 10 years. Event Info Sheet

Daily Schedule

Day 1: PSYCHOLOGY OF A LEADER

We lay the groundwork and begin the journey toward more effective leadership teaching you the fundamentals of human needs psychology. Develop an understanding of why people think, feel and behave the way they do. Learn the three mandates of leadership, and how to identify and utilize advanced communication styles to influence others and become an effective leader that can get real results.

Day 2: NAVIGATING CHANGE

Learn essential skills about non-verbal communication which accounts for 93% of the way we connect. Begin implementing and making practical, effective use of Neuro-Linguistic Programming with speaker Sid Jacobson, and implicit body language communications with speaker Jan Hargrave. Partner and team exercises will explore how you can obtain leverage by understanding the world of others, interrupt negative patterns and navigate opportunities for creating positive change.

Day 3: CREATING TRANSFORMATION

Focus more intensely on the power and importance of building teams and take a deeper dive into inspirational leadership with speaker Robyn Benicasa. Learn tools to create transformation and uncover breakthroughs for those you lead. Identify systematic beliefs and leverage framing techniques to create empowering alternatives to perceived problems, so you begin to lead others toward more positive outcomes and behavior.

Day 4: TESTING YOUR SKILLS

Assemble the strategies, tools and methods you've practiced and discover how to apply what you've learned with speaker Cloe Madanes. And discover the power within you a leader to create change and positively influence others. Expect surprises as your skills are put to the stress-test through trial runs and advanced exercises.
